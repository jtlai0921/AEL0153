1、 When there is a will, there is a way.	,	有志者事竟成。
2、 One who wishes to be good at his work should sharpen his tools first.	,	工欲善其事，必先利其器。
3、 Two heads are better than one.	,	三個臭皮匠，勝過諸葛亮。
4、 Rome was not built in a day.	,	羅馬不是一天造成的。
5、 Where there is life, there is hope.	,	留得青山在，不怕沒材燒。
6、 At birth we bring nothing; at death we take away nothing.	,	生不帶來，死不帶去。
7、A fool may talk, but a wise man speaks.	,	愚者多空談，智者言有物。
8、A rolling stone gathers no moss.	,	滾石不生苔，常轉業不聚財。
9、Bad news travels fast.	,	壞事傳千里。
10、A politician thinks of the next election; a statesman, of the next generation.	,	政客想的是下次選舉，政治家想的是下一代。
11、A leopard doesn’t change its spots.	,	江山易改，本性難移。
12、There’s no such thing as a free lunch.	,	 天下沒有白吃的午餐。
13、A friend in need is a friend indeed.	,	 患難見真情。 
14、Like father, like son.	,	 有其父必有其子。 
15、Knowledge is power.	,	 知識就是力量。
16、Heaven helps those who help themselves.	,	天助自助者。
17、There are other fish in the sea.	,	天涯何處無芳草。
18、The blood of the soldiers makes the glory of the general.	,	 一將功成萬骨枯。
19、Seeing is believing.	,	 百聞不如一見。
20、Haste makes waste.	,	 欲速則不達。
21、Every dog has his day.	,	行行出狀元。
22、After rain comes sunshine.	,	 苦盡甘來。
23、Better late than never.	,	亡羊補牢，時猶未晚。
24、Birds of a feather flock together.	,	物以類聚。
25、Business before pleasure.	,	先工作，後享樂。
26、Do in Rome as the Romans do.	,	入境隨俗。
27、Everything is good for something.	,	 天生我才必有用。
28、Absence makes the heart grow fonder. 	,	 小別勝新婚。
29、Actions speak louder than words. 	,	 行動勝於空談（事實勝於雄辯）。
30、Advice when most needed is least heeded. 	,	 忠言逆耳。
31、After a storm comes a calm. 	,	 否極泰來。
32、All good things come to an end. 	,	 花無百日紅（天下無不散之筵席）。
33、All is well that ends well. 	,	 結果佳者方佳。
34、All roads lead to Rome. 	,	 條條大道通羅馬（行行出狀元）
35、All that glitters is not gold. 	,	 閃礫者未必都是金（人不可貌相）。
36、All work and no play makes Jack a dull boy. 	,	 只工作不消遣，使人遲鈍。
37、All's well that ends well. 	,	 善終為善。
38、An eye for an eye and a tooth for a tooth. 	,	 以眼還眼，以牙還牙。
39、Art is long, life is short. 	,	 人生苦短，而學術無窮。
40、As you sow, so shall you reap. 	,	 種瓜得瓜，種豆得豆。
41、A bad penny always comes back. 	,	 惡有惡報。
42、A bad shearer never had a good sickle. 	,	 劣工尤器。
43、A bad workman always blames his tools. 	,	 劣工尤器。
44、A bird in the hand is worth two in the bush. 	,	 一鳥在手勝於兩鳥在林。
45、A burnt child dreads the fire. 	,	 受灼的小孩必怕火（一朝被蛇咬，十年怕草繩）。
46、A cat may look at a king. 	,	 君王卿相於我何。
47、A contented mind is a perpetual feast. 	,	 知足常樂。
48、A drowning man will catch at a straw. 	,	 急不暇擇。
49、A fault confessed is half redressed. 	,	 肯認錯是改過的一半。
50、A friend in need is a friend indeed. 	,	 患難之友，乃為真友。
51、A Jack of all trades is master of none. 	,	 樣樣通樣樣稀鬆（博藝無所精）。
52、A leopard cannot change his spots. 	,	 江山易改，本性難移。
53、A little learning is a dangerous thing. 	,	 一知半解最危險。
54、A little pot is soon hot. 	,	 小人物易發怒。
55、A man is known by the company he keeps. 	,	 觀其友，知其人。
56、A new broom sweeps clean. 	,	 新官上任三把火。
57、A penny saved is a penny earned. 	,	 省一文則賺一文。
58、A rolling stone gathers no moss. 	,	 轉石不生苔（轉業不聚財）。
59、A soft answer turneth away wrath. 	,	 和言足以息怒（柔足以克剛）
60、A sound mind in a sound body. 	,	 健全的頭腦寓於健全的身體。
61、A stitch in time saves nine. 	,	 及時行事，事半功倍。
62、A word spoken is past recalling. 	,	 一言既出，駟馬難追。
